About Moon Ticket

This is a token that will reward you for holding it, without having to manually stake it. Just sit back, relax, and watch your Moon Tickets automatically increase with every transaction! Each transaction triggers a 5% fee, where
2.5% is redistributed to all the current holders, in proportion to their current holdings. Diamond hands shall be rewarded! Additionally, 2.5% will be burned forever, therefore decreasing the total supply of the token as transactions are
made, making it more scarce as time passes. This inversely proportional relationship constitutes a supply and demand model. There is also no limit set as to how many tokens can be burnt.

How To Buy

How To Buy Moon Ticket

Wallet Setup

Download Metamask or Trustwallet to your computer or phone. Once installed, set up your wallet as you would any other wallet and continue to the next step.

In Metamask : Settings -> Networks -> Add network
Type in the following:
Network Name: Smart Chain
URL: https://bsc-dataseed.binance.org/
Chain ID: 56
Symbol: BNB
Block Explorer URL: https://bscscan.com/




PancakeSwap Buy
Send BNB to your wallet using Binance global or Binance.us
Before sending BNB make sure you click Binance Smart Chain (BSC, BEP20) version.
Go to:
https://exchange.pancakeswap.finance/#/swap?outputCurrency=0x073c15a67cB847661aB7284c55B20F413FCfd60f and you can swap your BNB for
Moon Ticket.

Sometimes you need to adjust the slippage (gear icon)

Troubleshooting
If you're having trouble connecting your wallet using pancake swap, access pancake swap using the metamask browser and connect your wallet within the meta mask browser. Input this link into the metamask browser to swap BNB to Moon Ticket.
Go to your wallet and scroll down to 'ADD TOKEN' and add this address: 0x073c15a67cB847661aB7284c55B20F413FCfd60f where it says Token Contract Address (the rest will fill itself in)
